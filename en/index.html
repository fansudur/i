About

Open your eyes, and the world exists. A world depends on who observes it, and from where; everything is sealed at the moment observation begins. From your first choice onward, you have had a light path of your own. Your motion is its time-slice through different spaces; your life, the stages and layers it has locked precisely in place; your world, the enclosure set as the light passed. What seems one self-evident world — one image — is layered and many-sided: visible to one another, unable to cross, unable to understand. And at this moment, you are standing at the only point where all of it holds.
